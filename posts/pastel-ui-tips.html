Pastel UI Tips

Sep 5, 2024 · 5 min read

DesignGuide

Pastels can be friendly and professional when balanced with strong typography and ample whitespace.

1. Pair with clear text colors

Use darker ink for text to maintain contrast. Our palette uses var(--text) and var(--primary-ink) to keep content readable.

2. Use layered backgrounds

Subtle gradients and a gentle grain add depth without clutter. Cards help contain content and improve scannability.

3. Keep components simple

Rounded corners, pill tags, and soft shadows reinforce the cozy vibe. Avoid overly saturated accents.

Start small, iterate, and validate with quick contrast checks.
